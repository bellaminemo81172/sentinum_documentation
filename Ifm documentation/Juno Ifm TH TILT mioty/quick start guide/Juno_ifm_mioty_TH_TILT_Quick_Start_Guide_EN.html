3. Sensor Activation with Magnet

There is a magnetic field switch on the sensor for easy activation. The following diagram shows the location of the magnetic field switch.

To activate the sensor, hold a standard magnet against the spot marked with an X. We recommend a neodymium magnet with a minimum surface area of 1 cm². The magnet must remain in place for at least 2 seconds until the device activates. This is confirmed by a beep.

4. Setting Up the Sensor via NFC

Activation is performed via an NFC app. A smartphone is required for this. The app can be downloaded from the respective app stores. Simply search for Sentinum LinQs and download the LinQs app.

First, locate the tag on the sensor and then the reader on your device.

The location of the tag is also marked on the top and labeled Tap here. You can also find the location of the NFC tag in the technical drawing.

Open the app and activate the sensor. To start the sensor in the basic settings, click the Activate Sensor button in the app start menu. Now place your device on the sensor NFC tag. When the sensor is activated, Sensor updated! will be displayed. You can then proceed to activate the other sensors.

Depending on the device manufacturer, the NFC readers are located in different places. Their location can be determined experimentally. Experience shows that they are either located higher up, as described in the image for iOS devices, or more toward the center for Android.
