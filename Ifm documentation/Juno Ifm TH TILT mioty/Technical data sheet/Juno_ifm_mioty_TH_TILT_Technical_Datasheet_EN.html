1. Overview

The Juno IP67 TH is a versatile temperature and humidity sensor featuring mioty® wireless technology for reliable and energy-efficient data transmission over long distances. Thanks to its robust construction and IP67 protection, it is ideal for use in demanding environments. Additionally, the sensor features tilt and damper opening detection with degree-precise adjustment.
