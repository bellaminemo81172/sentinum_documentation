2. Overview

The Juno IP69k ID/TILT is designed for reliable tilt and opening detection of flaps and doors. The sensor has no dedicated tracking mechanisms and must be tracked passively via the respective network. The Juno ID/TILT is optionally available with mioty® or LoRaWAN®. With a precise tilt sensor, it can reliably detect flap openings. Thanks to its robust design and IP69k rating, it is ideal for use in demanding environments and can withstand dust, water and high pressures.
