Overview

The solar-powered, wireless IoT sensor NYX is an innovative solution for monitoring indoor environmental parameters. With a thickness of only 8 mm and powered by indoor light, it offers a maintenance-free and visually appealing design. The sensor measures temperature, relative humidity, and ambient light. It is available with LoRaWAN® and mioty® communication in different configurations.

Key Features

  • Temperature, humidity and light intensity (ambient light indicator)
  • Ultra-flat design
  • mioty® and LoRaWAN® communication
  • Sustainable due to battery-free operation
  • No battery replacement required
  • Configurable parameters via NFC
  • Optimized antenna performance
  • Acoustic and visual feedback via LEDs and buzzer

Scope of Delivery

  • 1× Nyx Sensor
  • 2× adhesive strips for the wall mount
  • 1× wall mount

Application Examples

The Nyx sensor is ideal for monitoring indoor air quality in various environments, including:

  • Educational institutions: Ensuring a healthy indoor climate in classrooms and lecture halls.
  • Office buildings: Improving air quality to enhance employee well-being and productivity.
  • Residential spaces: Monitoring indoor air to promote comfort and health.
  • Public facilities: Ensuring compliance with air quality standards in libraries, museums, and other public spaces.

By continuously monitoring CO₂ levels and other environmental parameters, the Nyx sensor helps maintain a healthy indoor climate and enables timely responses to changes.
