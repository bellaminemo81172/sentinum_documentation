1. General Description

The JUNO Tracker is a versatile tracking device supporting mioty®, LoRaWAN®, as well as cellular technologies such as NB‑IoT and LTE‑CAT‑M1 for reliable and energy‑efficient data transmission.

The tracker is available in multiple versions, including variants specifically designed for cold‑chain monitoring.

Thanks to its robust design and IP69k / IP67 protection rating, the JUNO Tracker is ideally suited for operation in harsh environments and is resistant to dust, water, and high pressure.

2. Key Features

  • NB‑IoT and LTE‑CAT‑M1® communication
  • Tracking options:
    • GNSS
    • Wi‑Fi SSID scan
    • Cell‑ID location
    • Data must be decoded with in lat and lon, very energy efficient
    • Tracking preferences adjustable
  • Enclosure versions: IP69k (Tracker), IP67 (TH)
  • Operating temperature: −25 °C to +85 °C, IK07
  • Optional sensors (TH versions): Temperature & humidity
  • Temperature and relative humidity sensor with high accuracy (optional TH versions)
  • Tracking-on-activity feature
  • Magnetic switch
  • Can be activated with a magnet (no smartphone required) or via NFC or BLE
  • Configuration via downlinks, NFC or BLE
  • FOTA via BLE
  • Adjustable limit values
  • Replaceable AA cells (LiSOCl2 see Saft LS14500)
  • Acoustic signal
  • Vandalism detection (shock and vibration detection)
  • Versions with SIM card slot available

3. Typical Applications

  • Asset tracking
  • Temperature and humidity monitoring
  • Cold‑chain monitoring
  • Logistics and supply chain
  • Inventory monitoring of operating resources
  • Wildlife, livestock and domestic animal monitoring
  • Construction machinery monitoring
  • Agriculture and environmental monitoring
  • Theft protection
  • Proof of shipment of goods
  • Shock and vibration detection
