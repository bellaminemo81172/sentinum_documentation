2. Overview

The Juno Tracker is a versatile tracker that uses mioty®, LoRaWAN® or cellular technologies such as NB-IoT and LTE-CAT-M1 for reliable and energy-efficient data transmission. The tracker is also available in different versions, e.g. for cold chain monitoring. Thanks to its robust design and IP69k/IP67 rating, it is ideal for use in demanding environments and can withstand dust, water and high pressures.
